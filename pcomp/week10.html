motor thing assignment!

this device is intended to be an instrument-type thing. it uses two motors. the dc motor has a piece of a metrocard taped to it. it simply spins really quickly. the servo motor has a thick wire attached to it. at the end of a wire is another card. the rotational motion of the servo is converted to linear motion by the wire and the stack of legos. the servo motor moves the card in and out of the range of the spinning metrocard. when it's IN range, the cards hit each other and make a rhythmic sound. when it's OUT of range, there is no sound. if the metrocard spins at different speeds (when you change the ptentiometer), the rhythm will change slightly. I kept this device pretty basic, but it would be a pretty versatile instrument if you could change when and where the servo moves (for now, it is automatic). it would also be cool if the servo had multiple cards attached to it, so the rhythm could be a different pitch depending on which card is activated.
