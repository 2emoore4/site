Here's a demonstration of my phong shading implementation.

I found some example ambient/diffuse/specular values for different materials, so I used those. The sphere is supposed to be unpolished silver, the cylinder is unpolished copper, and the cube is black plastic.

You can kinda see seams between the faces, but I noticed that on Ken's presentation too, so I don't think it's an issue. It's much harder to notice with a higher polygon count.
